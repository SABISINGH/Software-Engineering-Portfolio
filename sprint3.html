What could I have done better?

Definitely should have saved my work on another device like USB, hard disk or pushed it to get-up or even to my gitlab account. Have done it now as per the images below. I tried merging my branch to development branch and had merged conflicts. I should have asked for help from Elise.




What lessons did I learn?

The biggest lesson I learnt during this sprint was to always save your work on multiple devices/locations as you never know what could go wrong. For me, pretty much everything went wrong half way through this sprint. Also learnt to Keep notes/workload when you install new hardware/software because then you can go back and follow the instructions If something goes wrong. Do not depend on others like I did for merging issues. I waited for Jonas as he offered to help me, but nothing happened even after asking the team multiple times. If I make mistakes (GitHub or software installation) try to fix it and if can't then I should ask Elise for help (For me this is the lesson learnt for life. It will also be helpful for me throughout this paper). As Elise said to me once that if I don't Make mistakes, then how will I learn how to fix them. This is the time I should make mistakes and learn from them.
